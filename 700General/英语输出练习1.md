## 整篇

### 纠错版

Everyone has their own habits that make them different. There are many small habits that can make our lives better.

In my life, I like getting up early, which lets me have more time to do meaningful things. For example, I cook a healthy breakfast by myself and study English. Besides, I like checking everything when I leave home. Because of this habit, I avoid many problems. Lastly, I always read books before sleeping, which helps me have a wonderful and peaceful sleep.

I think all of us should develop good habits. Although some habits are small, they can help us live a better life.

### 提升版

Everyone possesses unique habits that distinguish them from others. Numerous tiny habits can significantly improve our quality of life.

Personally, I am in the habit of getting up early, which grants me extra time to do meaningful things. For instance, I prepare a nutritious breakfast on my own and devote time to studying English. Additionally, I make it a rule to double-check all items before leaving home. Thanks to this habit, I have avoided many potential troubles. Finally, I consistently read books before going to bed, which enables me to enjoy a sound and peaceful sleep.

I hold the view that all of us ought to cultivate beneficial habits. Even though some habits seem trivial, they can substantially contribute to a better life.
